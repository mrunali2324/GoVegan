Summer Vegetable Tian

Prep Time : 20 mins

Total time : 1hr 10 mins

Servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 3 small onions
  • 1 medium summer squash
  • 1 medium zucchini
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Directions

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

Step 2: Heat oil and garlic in a small saucepan over medium-high heat until the garlic begins to sizzle, about 1 minute. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3:Slice tomatoes, onions, squash and zucchini into 1/8-inch-thick slices. Standing them up on their sides, alternate the tomato, onion, squash and zucchini slices in a circular pattern around the edge of a 9-inch deep-dish glass pie pan or similar-size round casserole dish. Make a second alternating circle in the center. Pack the vegetables tightly: they will shrink while baking.

Step 4: Discard the garlic, if desired, and drizzle the vegetables with the oil. Sprinkle with salt. Bake until the vegetables are tender and starting to brown, about 1 hour. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving

Zucchini Tomato Pasta

Prep Time : 40 mins

Total time : 1hr 10 mins

Servings: 5

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (such as cheddar or mozzarella), optional
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish, optional

Directions

Step 1:Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

Step 3:Add diced carrots, celery, bell pepper, and zucchini to the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

Step 4: Stir in the cherry tomatoes, chickpeas, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Step 5:

Pour vegetable broth into the skillet and stir to combine all the ingredients. Let simmer for about 5 minutes until the broth reduces slightly.

Step 6: Transfer the vegetable mixture into a casserole dish. If using grated cheese, sprinkle it evenly over the top of the casserole.

Step 7: Cover the casserole dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

Step 8:Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes until the cheese (if using) is melted and bubbly.

Step 9:Garnish with fresh parsley or basil if desired before serving.

Step 10:Serve hot as a main dish or a side dish. Enjoy your Chickpea and Vegetable Casserole!

Quinoa and Black beans

Prep Time : 15 mins

Total time : 50 mins

Servings: 10

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • ¾ cup quinoa
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.

Step 2: Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.

Step 3:Mix quinoa into onion mixture and cover with vegetable broth; season with cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.

Step 4: Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer until quinoa is tender and broth is absorbed, about 20 minutes.

Step 5: Stir in frozen corn and continue to simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes. Mix in black beans and cilantro.

Garlic Zuccini Stir Fry

Prep Time : 5 mins

Total time : 20 mins

Servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 large sweet onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 medium zucchini, halved and sliced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 pinch Italian seasoning, or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1: Heat oil and garlic in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in hot oil-garlic mixture until onion is almost translucent, about 3 to 5 minutes. Add zucchini; cook and stir until zucchini is slightly crisp, but not mushy, about 10 minutes.

Step 2: Stir in diced tomatoes and cook until heated through. Stir in Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.

Lemon Fried Rice

Prep Time : 20 mins

Total time : 1hr 10 mins

Servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Rice, cooked
  • 1 tsp Urad dal / Ulundu Paruppu
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds / Kaduku
  • 1 tsp Channa Dal / Kadala Paruppu
  • 2 Dry Red Chillies
  • 1 sprig Curry leaves
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder / Manjal Podi
  • to taste Salt
  • 1 Lemon
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Directions

Step 1: Heat oil in a kadai. Add mustard seeds, channa dal, urad dal, dry red chillies, curry leaves and fry for 30 seconds.

Step 2: Now add in salt and turmeric powder and fry for 10 seconds.

Step 3: Add lemon juice and mix well.

Step 4: Immediately add cooked rice and mix well. Stir until everything is heated through. Serve hot or put in a lunch box along with potato poriyal.

Lettuce Salad Cup

Prep Time : 20 mins

Total time : 1hr 10 mins

Servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Cucumbers, chopped
  • Tomatoes, chopped
  • Bell peppers, chopped
  • Any other desired vegetables, chopped (e.g., carrots, radishes)
  • Cooked quinoa or grains of your choice
  • Your favorite salad dressing
  • Fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)
  • Nuts or seeds (optional, for garnish)

Directions

Step 1: Wash and dry the lettuce leaves.

Step 2: Prepare the salad ingredients by chopping cucumbers, tomatoes, bell peppers, and any other desired vegetables.

Step 3: In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped vegetables with cooked quinoa or any other desired grains.

Step 4: Add your favorite dressing to the salad mixture and toss until well coated.

Step 5: Take individual lettuce leaves and fill each one with the salad mixture.

Step 6: Garnish with fresh herbs, nuts, or seeds if desired.

Step 7: Serve the lettuce salad cups immediately as an appetizer or light meal.

Spinach Lentil Curry

Prep Time : 15 mins

Total time : 1hr /h2>

Servings: 5

INGREDIENTS

  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 3 small onions
  • 1 medium summer squash
  • 1 medium zucchini
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Directions

Step 1: Rinse the lentils (such as red lentils or yellow lentils) under cold water until the water runs clear.

Step 2: In a pot, combine the rinsed lentils with water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until lentils are tender, about 15-20 minutes.

Step 3: Meanwhile, heat oil in a separate pan over medium heat.

Step 4: Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Sauté until they start to splutter.

Step 5: Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are soft and translucent.

Step 6: Stir in ground turmeric, ground coriander, ground cumin, and chili powder. Cook for a minute until fragrant.

Step 7: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down and become mushy.

Step 8: Once the lentils are cooked, add them to the pan with the onion-tomato mixture.

Step 9: Add chopped spinach leaves and stir until wilted.

Step 10: Pour in coconut milk or vegetable broth to adjust the consistency of the curry.

Step 11: Season with salt to taste and let the curry simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 12: Garnish with chopped cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.

Step 13: Serve hot with rice or naan bread.

Mexican Fajitas

Prep Time : 10 mins

Total time : 1hr /h2>

Servings: 7

INGREDIENTS

  • Sliced steak, chicken, shrimp, or tofu (for vegetarian option)
  • Bell peppers (red, green, and/or yellow), sliced
  • Onion, sliced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chili powder
  • Ground cumin
  • Paprika
  • Tortillas (flour or corn)

Directions

Step 1:Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Add sliced onions and bell peppers to the skillet. Cook until they are softened and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes.

Step 3: Meanwhile, heat oil in a separate pan over medium heat.

Step 4:Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the sliced steak, chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the empty side. Cook until browned and cooked through..

Step 5: Season the meat and vegetables with minced garlic, salt, pepper, chili powder, ground cumin, and paprika. Stir to combine..

Drizzle lime juice over the mixture and stir again.

Warm tortillas in a separate skillet or in the microwave.

Serve the fajita filling in warm tortillas, and top with optional toppings as desired.

Roll up the tortillas and enjoy!
